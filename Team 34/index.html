What is Organ Donation?

 

  • It is the process of removing organ or tissue from a live,dead or recently dead person to be used for another person
  • The person who donates is donor and who recieves is recipient.
  • Organs like lungs, heart,liver, kidneys, intestines and pancreas
  • Tissues like comea, skin, bone marrow, heart valves middle ear can be restored
